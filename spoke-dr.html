Standby workload spoke

spoke-dr

Decision point

Standby semantics

The main open design question is whether spoke-dr should remain platform standby or become app-ready hot standby. If app-ready standby is required, app placement, OpenShift secrets, routes, mesh labels, backup policy, and validation should be added to lab-workloads deliberately.

Current assumption: platform standby, not full hot workload mirror.
