Roadmap and gates

The authoritative resume queue remains in TODO.md. This page gives the project-readable version of the same operational sequence.

1

Fresh ACM backup

Wait for or create a fresh hub backup that meets the agreed drill RPO.

2

Image readiness

Mirror or pre-pull ACM/MCE images required by hub-dr restore startup.

3

Activation preflight

Prove hub-dr passive state, dry-run restore manifests, and restate abort criteria.

4

Controlled drill

Run hub-dc to hub-dr activation only after gates pass and ownership risk is accepted.
